# DELICIOUS IN DUNGEON · DUN

> A sphere of **UD0** — the ROOT0 universe / biosphere. Domain: **ENTERTAINMENT**.

DELICIOUS IN DUNGEON (DUN) — Studio Trigger 2024, from Ryoko Kui's manga 'Dungeon Meshi'. A broke party (Laios, Marcille, Chilchuck, joined by the dwarf Senshi) descends to revive Laios's sister Falin from a Red Dragon — and, with no supplies, survives by COOKING AND EATING THE MONSTERS, each dish a rigorous study of the labyrinth's ecology. TROPES: food as the lens on ecology/economics/care ('know your world' as a moral), hunger & ambition, a found family learning to feed each other. Trigger in patient, character-driven mode. Render-not-invent + IP-clean. LIVE descending-dungeon interactive. Vellum + dungeon-green + quorum-ring favicon. 8 emergents.
